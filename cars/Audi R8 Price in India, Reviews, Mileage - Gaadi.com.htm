Editorial Test Drive Review of Audi R8

 Audi has launched the new R8 in India. In the new R8 the exterior design has been honed ever further. The new R8 now has all-LED headlights and rear lights are standard with all models. The singleframe grille is now tapered corners at the top plus you get the redesigned diffuser and the large, round tailpipes are the design changes. The rear lights of all models now have turn signals. The interior is also subtly changed. With the optional honeycomb-quilted Fine Nappa full-leather package, the seat covers and inserts in the door liners are quilted. This quilting is also available as an option for the Alcantara headlining. The Audi R8 V8 delivers 316 kW (430 hp) and 430 Nm of torque between 4,500 and 6,000 rpm. In combination with the seven-speed S tronic, it accelerates the R8 Coupé from zero to 100 km/h in 4.3 seconds on its way to a top speed of 300 km/h. The Audi R8 V10 Coupé with the S tronic does that in 3.6 seconds and has a top speed of 314 km/h and delivers a massive 525 hp. The new Audi R8 V10 Spyder does that 3.8 seconds with a top speed of 311 km/h. All versions of the R8 feature an axle load distribution of roughly 43:57  which is crucial for a sportscar. Dry sump lubrication enables the engines to be mounted low, giving the vehicle a very low center of gravity. All of the wheels are mounted on aluminum double wishbones. The setup is tuned for high dynamics, yet delivers good comfort. The adaptive damping system Audi magnetic ride (standard in the R8 V10 Coupé) varies the response of the shock absorbers as neede. The ride of the new R8 is firm but not uncomfortable. The chassis of the Audi R8 V10 is decidedly tuned for dynamics.Depending on the engine, the alloy wheels measure 18 or 19 inches in diameter, the latter in a new 5 twin-spoke design. The brake system now has four discs are internally vented and perforated, and their wave design reduces the weight. Audi also offers optional carbon fiber-ceramic discs that are even better. These come standard with the R8 V10 plus. Handling is just brilliant and the new R8 also has many safety features. The new R8 is priced from Rs 1.35 crore onwards. For a detailed analysis read below
